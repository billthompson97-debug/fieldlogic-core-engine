# Adaptive Operational Learning

## Purpose

FieldLogic is evolving from static operational recommendations into adaptive operational learning.

## Core concept

The runtime should continuously evaluate:
- whether recommendations were applied
- whether callbacks decreased
- whether QA improved
- whether labor variance improved
- whether homeowner experience improved
- whether margin leakage improved

## Why this matters

Without feedback learning:
- recommendations stagnate
- reasoning plateaus
- anomaly detection weakens
- operational guidance remains static

With adaptive learning:
- recommendations evolve
- runtime interpretation improves
- operational guidance strengthens
- benchmarking improves
- operational reasoning compounds

## Strategic significance

This creates:
- operational feedback loops
- continuously improving operational guidance
- evolving operational intelligence
- adaptive operational cognition

## Long-term objective

FieldLogic should eventually function as a continuously learning operational intelligence system that improves operational guidance through real-world remodeling outcomes.
