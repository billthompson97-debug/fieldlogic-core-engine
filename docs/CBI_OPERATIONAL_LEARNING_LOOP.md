# CBI Operational Learning Loop

## Purpose

CBI serves as the live operational proving ground for FieldLogic.

The objective is to continuously generate structured operational telemetry that improves:
- replay quality
- benchmark quality
- anomaly detection
- operational reasoning
- recommendation accuracy
- runtime evaluation

## Core telemetry streams

### Callback telemetry
Tracks:
- homeowner concerns
- callback categories
- installer linkage
- QA linkage
- resolution outcomes

### QA telemetry
Tracks:
- QA findings
- punch items
- waterproofing reviews
- callback probability
- remediation pathways

### Installer telemetry
Tracks:
- labor variance
- QA performance
- callback rate
- photo compliance
- homeowner experience

### Margin telemetry
Tracks:
- schedule drift
- labor overages
- material variance
- margin leakage
- profitability drivers

## Strategic significance

The more operational telemetry captured:
- the stronger replay becomes
- the stronger benchmarking becomes
- the stronger reasoning becomes
- the stronger recommendations become

## Long-term objective

CBI becomes the operational intelligence training ground for FieldLogic.
