# CBI Telemetry Capture Standard

## Purpose

Standardize operational telemetry collection across California Bathrooms, Inc. projects.

## Core telemetry categories

### Tech Measure
Capture:
- scope type
- slab condition
- plumbing relocation requirements
- material system
- scope risk score
- required field photos

### Production
Capture:
- installer assignments
- planned vs actual hours
- labor variance
- delay reasons
- material shortages
- phase completion timestamps

### QA
Capture:
- QA scores
- unresolved punch items
- waterproofing findings
- photo compliance
- callback probability
- required remediation actions

### Callback
Capture:
- homeowner concern
- callback category
- days after completion
- installer linkage
- labor variance history
- initial QA score

### Margin Signals
Capture:
- labor variance
- material variance
- schedule delay
- estimated margin leak
- primary profitability drivers

## Strategic significance

Consistent telemetry collection creates:
- operational replay fidelity
- benchmark quality
- anomaly detection accuracy
- recommendation quality
- operational learning loops

## Long-term objective

CBI becomes the operational proving ground and telemetry generation engine for FieldLogic.
