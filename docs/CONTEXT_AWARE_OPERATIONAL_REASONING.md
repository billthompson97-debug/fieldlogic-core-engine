# Context-Aware Operational Reasoning

## Purpose

Context-aware operational reasoning allows FieldLogic to synthesize operational signals, historical memory, relationships, benchmarking, and predictive intelligence into actionable operational understanding.

This is the transition from:
- operational tracking
- operational analytics

into:
- operational reasoning
- operational guidance
- operational intelligence

## Core context layers

### Telemetry
Operational events captured across systems.

### Lineage
Cause-and-effect operational chains.

### Replay
Chronological operational reconstruction.

### State
Live operational project condition.

### Benchmarking
Cross-project comparative intelligence.

### Graph Relationships
Contextual operational relationships.

### Memory
Historical operational knowledge retrieval.

### Prediction
Forecasted operational risk and outcomes.

## Why this matters

Most construction software answers:
- what happened?

FieldLogic is evolving toward answering:
- why did it happen?
- what is likely to happen next?
- what should operations do now?

## Long-term objective

FieldLogic eventually becomes a continuously learning operational intelligence layer capable of supporting:
- production leadership
- field operations
- QA teams
- installer coaching
- scheduling
- homeowner experience management
- profitability protection
