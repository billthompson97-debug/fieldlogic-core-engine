# Job Health Model

## Purpose

The Job Health Model acts as the operational heartbeat for every remodeling project.

Instead of relying on fragmented KPIs, FieldLogic consolidates operational signals into a continuously evolving health score.

## Core signals

- scope risk
- QA quality
- labor variance
- schedule delays
- callback activity
- homeowner experience
- margin exposure

## Why this matters

Most remodeling companies discover problems after:
- callbacks occur
- labor is blown
- schedules collapse
- homeowners become frustrated
- profitability disappears

FieldLogic is designed to detect operational deterioration early.

## Health classifications

### Healthy
Project operating within expected standards.

### Watch
Minor operational drift detected.

### At Risk
Multiple operational signals degrading.

### Intervention Required
Project likely requires management escalation.

## Long-term objective

The Job Health Model eventually becomes:
- a predictive operational warning system
- a scheduling prioritization engine
- a production escalation layer
- a margin protection system
- a homeowner experience safeguard
