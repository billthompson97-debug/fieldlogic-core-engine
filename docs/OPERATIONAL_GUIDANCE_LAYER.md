# Operational Guidance Layer

## Purpose

The Operational Guidance Layer transforms FieldLogic from a passive reporting platform into an active operational intelligence system.

Instead of only showing operational conditions, the system begins recommending intervention pathways.

## Core functions

- operational recommendations
- escalation guidance
- coaching recommendations
- callback prevention actions
- anomaly response guidance
- production prioritization

## Why this matters

Most remodeling software reports problems after operational damage has already occurred.

FieldLogic is designed to:
- detect risk early
- surface root causes
- recommend intervention
- protect homeowner experience
- defend operational margin

## Strategic significance

The guidance layer becomes the bridge between:
- telemetry
- intelligence
- operational action

This creates the foundation for:
- AI operational copilots
- automated interventions
- production optimization
- operational decision support

## Long-term vision

FieldLogic should eventually function as an operational intelligence partner capable of continuously identifying, prioritizing, and recommending actions across residential remodeling operations.
