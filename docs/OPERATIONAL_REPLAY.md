# Operational Replay System

## Purpose

The Operational Replay System reconstructs the chronological operational history of a remodeling project.

Instead of reviewing disconnected notes, FieldLogic rebuilds the actual operational sequence.

## Why this matters

Most remodeling failures are not isolated.

They are chains:
- scope gaps
- ordering mistakes
- installer mismatch
- QA misses
- communication breakdowns
- scheduling drift

Traditional systems hide these relationships.

FieldLogic reconstructs them.

## Core capabilities

- chronological event replay
- operational timeline reconstruction
- failure chain visualization
- callback root-cause tracing
- QA event analysis
- labor variance propagation
- margin erosion analysis

## Strategic importance

Operational replay becomes the foundation for:
- AI-assisted diagnostics
- installer coaching
- production optimization
- operational benchmarking
- predictive intelligence

## Long-term objective

FieldLogic should eventually identify recurring operational patterns across thousands of remodeling projects and surface early warning signals before failure occurs.
